ABOUT

MYSELF

Greetings! My name is Rosie Ellis and I'm a passionate UI and UX desinger who is also a web developer from New York City. My experience has taught me that good design is not created in a moment of inspiration or a bubble. It is an iterative process requiring collaboration between multiple teams and your end users.
It is easy to get attached to initial design solutions and forgo research and testing but this rarely works. First solutions are often wrong because in the eraly stages it is hard to know enough about the problem you are attempting to solve.
